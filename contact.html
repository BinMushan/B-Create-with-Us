Frequently asked questions

What is your design process?

My design process usually starts with understanding your design brief and requirements. Then, I do research on your brand, target audience, and competitors to come up with design concepts. After that, I create rough sketches and refine them into digital drafts. Finally, I present the design options to you for feedback and make revisions accordingly.

How long will it take to complete the project?

The project's timeline varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. I can give you an estimated timeframe after understanding the project requirements. However it usually doesn't take more than 2-3 weeks to finish your entire project, sometimes shorter than that if your lucky.

Can you work within my budget?

I completely understand your budget constraints, and I will work with you to find a solution that delivers the best possible design within your budget. My goal is to provide you with the best value while ensuring that the design meets your needs and exceeds your expectations.

What happens if I don't like the designs I get?

This is a common concern, and that is precisely why revision rounds are an essential part of the design process. With your feedback, we will work together to refine the design until it meets your vision and goals. My ultimate goal is to create a design that you love and are proud to use.

How do revisions work?

When it comes to revisions rounds, my approach is to provide my clients with the highest level of customization and flexibility possible. I will basically provide you with the presentation suitable to your package and will try to collect all your thoughts and ideas to implement all possible changes to come closer to your vision.

Do you offer website design?

I do provide website designs, and I know pretty talented guys that I work with, and together we will make sure that you receive a modern and professional website specially designed for your brand, to showcase your values and give you a stunning online presence.
